Income Inequality

Income inequality refers to the unequal distribution of income among individuals or households within a society. It is often measured by indicators such as the Gini coefficient, which quantifies the extent to which income distribution deviates from perfect equality.

Lack of Access to Basic Needs.

Millions of people around the world face challenges in accessing basic necessities for survival, including food, water, and shelter. This lack of access contributes to widespread poverty and human suffering.
